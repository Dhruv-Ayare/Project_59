My favorite hobby

Gaming

My favorite hobby is gaming because you can play on the computer and relax. My favorite game to play is Minecraft. Minecraft is a sandbox game where you can build, survive, and adventure. I like building in the game because I can use my imagination. I can build cities, castles, and vehicles. I forgot to mention that the one aspect that makes this game unique is that the whole world is made of cubes. There are animals, trees, oceans and shipwrecks. There is much more but I would be going on and on. So overall gaming on Minecraft is my favorite hobby.
